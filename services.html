OUR SERVICES

Vitality Health Checks

The Vitality Health Check is a simple and convenient set of important health screening and preventive tests.

Antenatal Classes

We provide Antenatal classes that can help you and your partner to prepare for labour, birth and early parenthood.

4D Scans

The 4D scan is like a 3D scan but gives you a real-time 3 dimensional moving image.

Baby and Adult Vaccinations

Baby and adult vaccination is the administration of a vaccine to help the immune system develop protection from a disease.

Voluntary Counseling and Testing

VCT is when a person chooses to undergo HIV/AIDS counselling so that they can make an informed decision about whether to be tested for HIV.

Primary Health Consultation

Primary care consultations involve regular check-ups with a primary care physician, also commonly known as a general practitioner or a family doctor.

First Aid Courses

First Aid course which is ideal for parents and aupairs whose role includes caring and looking after premature babies, infants, toddlers and young children.

Corporate Wellness

Focus on employee health and wellness to get your employees to engage in healthy behavior as a way to reduce healthcare and health insurance costs.

Weight Check-ups

We check your child's weight and height, calculate body mass index (BMI), maintaining a healthy weight range helps prevent longer-term diseases.
