OUR SERVICES

Baby and Adult Vaccinations

Make the right choice and choose to vaccinate. Protect your loved ones and help to boost their immune system.

Weight Check-ups

We check your child's weight, height, head circumference and advise on milestones to ensure your baby's health and well-being.

Antenatal Classes

We will assist you and your partner to prepare for the beautiful journey of parenthood.

4D Scans

An experience that one should never miss. Get to meet your baby before he/she arrives. See your baby in real time 4D imaging.

Primary Health Care Consultation

Feeling blue? Do you have a minor ailment? Get your regular check-ups with our primary health care nurse.

First Aid Courses

Empower yourself with life saving skills. We offer first aid courses for parents and caregivers to ensure that you are always ready for any emergency.

Vitality Health Checks

Get your health screening and preventative tests done with us. Be healthy, stay healthy.

Corporate Wellness

A healthy workforce, is a more productive workforce. Stay healthy and prevent chronic diseases by having your regular health assessments done with us. We will come to you.

Voluntary Counseling and Testing

Make an informed decision, get tested and know your status.
