ABOUT US

Background

Established in the year 2018

Driven by passion for maternal and child health

Nicole Tshimanga Incorporated was started up by Sister Nicole Tshimanga who has an immense passion for working with people and believes in the holistic management of her patients. Trading as DonVic Family Practice. The trading name is of great significance with a meaning of ‘God given Victory’. Sr Nicole Tshimanga holds an Honours Nursing Degree from Wits University with specialties in Midwifery, Community Health Nursing, Psychiatric Nursing and General Nursing. She is an aspiring leader in her field and her work experience has allowed her to cover many of the bases to offer full optimum health care services.

What We Seek To Do

We seeks to contribute to some key millennium development goals: reducing child mortality through vaccination and improving maternal health through adequate antenatal screening and education. It seeks to leave footprints in the hearts of all it’s patients and acts on a beautiful quote from Maya Angelou “They may forget your name, but they will never forget how you made them feel”.

Mother and Baby Clinic

Having worked at a Mother and Baby Clinic, she was able to express her passion for maternal and child health, and excelled in the process. The experience gained was invaluable and the realization of her dream to start her own practice can into view.

Strong Believes

DonVic Family Practice strongly believes in the holistic care of every patient in order to allow complete healing.

Committed and Excited

DonVic Family practice is committed and excited to be a part of yours and your family's wellbeing journey.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

We seek to contribute to the millennium development goals by reducing child mortality and morbidity rates through vaccinations and imparting the importance thereof, improving maternal health through adequate antenatal screening and education by providing informative and interactive antenatal classes, and thus by providing holistic health care services for families.

Our
Vision

We endeavor to provide quality health care services with compassion and dedication, through collaboration of a multidisciplinary team, and ensuring the holistic wellbeing of every individual
